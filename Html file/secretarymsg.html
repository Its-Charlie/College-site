Secretary's Message

Saraswati Institute Of Technology | Secretary's Message:

Dear Students,
The improvement in the field of technology has quickened the interest in esteem-based engineering training which is subjective, dynamic, multidimensional, and comprehensively serious. We give the studies a perfect learning condition through intelligent study hall, educating instructional exercises, lab practicals, Industrial visits, and talks by industry specialists for the modern presentation of students and that Make Us One of the Reputed Polytechnic Colleges in Navi Mumbai. Relentless endeavors are being made by our placement group to put our students in empowering and decent situations in the business. The aim of the college’s Placement Cell is to provide 100% placement to all students. SIOT has been established with a vision to become one of the Reputed Polytechnic Colleges in Navi Mumbai. For this, the Cell related to the Soft Skills office holds mock meetings and gathering conversations all the time. The help given to the students at the pre-placement stage prepares them tremendously and helps them in accommodating their line of interest. It is in fact a matter of great pride for us that the leading corporate houses and industrial set-ups have always looked towards SIOT for quality manpower and have repeatedly visited the campus to recruit our students. Our intention is to make our students, not only employable but also ‘Industry Ready’, having the latest practical exposure to fit into their prospective job profiles successfully. We want our students to enjoy all that life has to offer to exceed expectations in and develop as pioneers and trailblazers in their picked lines of calling. Learning should be founded on getting things done and not simply knowing things. Until and except if learning arrangements identify with reality and propel the student to obtain and apply the information, the entire procedure will stay shallow. Any engineering organization worth its name hopes to streamline the productivity of worldwide pioneers. Our organization has set objectives targets and arranged activities for accomplishing excellence in all circles of technical education.

Mrs. Vrushali P. Deshmukh (Secretary, SES)
Secretary
