Central Library

The Central Library of SIOT Kharghar was established in 2008. The library is semi-computerized from its inception. A wide variety of textbooks, handbooks, and national journals are available in the Reference Section. The Central Library at Top Polytechnic Colleges in Mumbai- SIOT provides a wide variety of textbooks, handbooks.


Library gives Internet, E-books, and Newspapers to all users. E-journal facility is also available providing a competitive edge to the students and staff.


The library is open from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm giving access to all the students, staff, and other office staff of the lobby, reference segment, and stack spaces.


In the entirety of our connections, we are guided by our fundamental beliefs like Knowledge, Service, Quality, Integrity, Respect, and Communication, goodness, discipline, responsibility, collaboration, and critical thinking.
